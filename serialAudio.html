Welcome to this instrument

To connect to the device, click anywhere and a drop down will appear letting you select the board.


Must wear headphones!

Detailed Instructions:
This device allows you to create and save music note by note.

Tap the big pad on the left, the notes will increase. 
To save the note, press the second button (small square).
To play back the whole sequence, click the triangle button.
To delete, click the X (the tune will play one last time).

The rightmost button changes the mode back and forth. 
In the second mode, the device will play a random note and you will try to match it.
Use the same big pad to find a note,
and submit it with the second button.
Hear the target note again by clicking the triangle.
